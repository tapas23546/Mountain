Jamshedpur Hills

Welcome to our Jamshedpur City hills! Enjoy the beauty and serenity of nature as you explore our hilly terrain. Have a wonderful stay!

Dalma Hill

Dalma Hill is a famous hill located on the outskirts of Jamshedpur. It is a popular destination for trekking and hiking enthusiasts as it provides a scenic and challenging trekking trail. At the top of the hill, there is a small temple dedicated to the Hindu deity, Lord Shiva. The hill also has a wildlife sanctuary, which is home to various species of animals and birds, including elephants, leopards, and peafowls.

Prashant Hill

Prashant Hill is a popular hill located on the outskirts of Jamshedpur. It is a well-known destination for locals and tourists alike, who come here for trekking, picnics, and to enjoy the panoramic views of the city. At the top of the hill, there is a small temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, which is a major attraction for visitors. Prashant Hill is a great place to spend a day in nature and to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

Ghatsila Hill

Ghatsila Hill is a scenic hill located in the outskirts of Jamshedpur, known for its natural beauty and breathtaking views. It is a popular destination for nature lovers, trekkers, and adventure enthusiasts. The hill is surrounded by dense forests and waterfalls, which adds to its charm. At the top of the hill, there is a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, which attracts many visitors. Ghatsila Hill is a must-visit place for those who want to experience the serenity and beauty of nature.

Netarhat

Netarhat Hill is a popular tourist destination located in the state of Jharkhand, India. It is situated at an altitude of around 3,700 feet and is known for its stunning natural beauty. The hill station is surrounded by dense forests, waterfalls, and streams, which attract many nature enthusiasts, trekkers, and adventure seekers. Netarhat is also famous for its breathtaking views of the sunrise and sunset. The hill station is a perfect escape for those who want to relax and rejuvenate amidst nature's beauty.
